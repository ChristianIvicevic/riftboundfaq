## What to expect [#what-to-expect]

Unleashed introduces a number of new mechanics and card interactions that require clarification in the official rules. Until v1.3 of the Core Rules Document is available, rulings on many of these questions can't be written up with the same level of citation and confidence that the rest of this FAQ provides.

If you have a rules question about an Unleashed card right now, your best options are:

- Refer to the current [Core Rules Document](https://riftbound.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/news/rules-and-releases/gameplay-guide-core-rules/) and apply the existing rules as best you can
- Check back here after March 30 when CRD v1.3 is released and FAQ entries begin rolling out
