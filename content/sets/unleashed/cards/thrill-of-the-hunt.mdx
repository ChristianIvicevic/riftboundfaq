## Can I use Thrill of the Hunt to score on my opponent's turn by banishing my sole unit at a controlled battlefield and replaying it there? [#score-on-opponents-turn]

No.

When Thrill of the Hunt resolves, the replayed unit is added to the chain as a pending item,<Rule number="354.2" /> with its play process held until the spell has fully resolved.<Rule number="354.3" />
Once the spell finishes resolving, a cleanup runs — but the pending unit keeps the chain alive throughout it.
Because the chain is not empty, the turn remains in a closed state, and cleanup step 4 — which would make the battlefield uncontrolled — only applies during an open state.<Rule number="323.6" />
Control is never lost, so there is no opportunity to score a point.
When all outstanding tasks are complete, the pending unit is finalized<Rule number="335.2" /> and replayed to the battlefield you still control.
