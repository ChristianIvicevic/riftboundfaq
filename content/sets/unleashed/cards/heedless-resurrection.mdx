## If Heedless Resurrection is countered, is the killed unit returned? [#no-refunds]

No.

Killing a friendly unit is an additional cost required to play Heedless Resurrection.
A countered spell does nothing and is not considered to have been played (<Rule number="412.1.a" />, <Rule number="412.1.b" />).
Countering does not refund any costs paid to play a card, including additional costs (<Rule number="412.1.c" />, <Rule number="412.1.c.1" />).
That cost is paid before the spell resolves, so the killed unit is not returned.
