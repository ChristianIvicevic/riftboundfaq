## Does Sunken Temple trigger if I conquer with a unit that is only Mighty due to Assault? [#mighty-from-assault]

Yes, Sunken Temple will trigger even if the unit is only <Mighty /> because of the bonus might it gained from <Assault />.

Control is established and the conquer occurs<Rule number="461.5.d" /> before attacker and defender designations are removed.<Rule number="461.7.a" />
This means units still have their attacker designation — and therefore <Assault /> active — at the time the conquer trigger is evaluated.
