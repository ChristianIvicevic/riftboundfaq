## Do opponents have to pay resources to play cards from Hidden while Vex is in combat? [#hidden-cost-increase]

Yes.
Cards played from Hidden by your opponents cost <Energy value={1} /><Power type="universal" /> when Vex is in combat.

When a card with Hidden is played from its facedown state, it is played "ignoring its base cost" (<Rule number="737.1.b" />).
During step 3 of playing a card ("Determine Total Cost"), the following happens in order:

1. Set base costs to zero (<Rule number="353.1" />): If an ability or instruction allows you to "ignore" costs, set the base cost(s) to zero. Hidden cards played from facedown have their base cost set to <Energy value={0} />.
2. Apply cost increases (<Rule number="353.3" />): Vex's ability increases enemy spell costs by <Energy value={1} /><Power type="universal" />. This is applied to the total cost even though the base cost is zero
3. Apply discounts (<Rule number="353.4" />): No discounts apply in this scenario

The total cost ends up being <Energy value={1} /><Power type="universal" /> because cost increases are applied after base costs are set to zero.
