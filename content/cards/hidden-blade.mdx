## Does the target's controller draw if the unit isn't killed? [#draw-if-not-killed]

Yes, as long as the target is still legal when Hidden Blade resolves.

Hidden Blade's draw instruction checks "its controller" — information about the target.
Because the target is still legal when Hidden Blade resolves, that information is accessible and the draw executes normally, regardless of whether the kill succeeded.<Rule number="359.3.e.12.a" />

<Callout type="idea" title="Example">
	You play Hidden Blade targeting an opponent's unit.
	They respond with [Zhonya's Hourglass](https://riftbound.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/card-gallery/#card-gallery--ogn-077-298), which replaces the unit's death.
	The unit is not killed, but because it was still a legal target when Hidden Blade resolved, the opponent still draws 2.
</Callout>

## Does the controller draw if the target becomes illegal? [#draw-if-illegal]

No, as an illegal target is entirely unaffected by the spell, and the draw is no exception.

An illegal target is unaffected by the spell as it resolves.<Rule number="359.3.e.5" />
Hidden Blade's draw instruction checks "its controller" — information about the target — but because the target is now illegal, that check returns null and the draw is ignored as well.<Rule number="359.3.e.12" />

<Callout type="idea" title="Example">
	You play Hidden Blade targeting an opponent's unit at a battlefield.
	They respond by moving it to their base using [Flash](https://riftbound.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/card-gallery/#card-gallery--ogs-011-024).
	Hidden Blade resolves, but the unit is no longer at a battlefield, making it an illegal target.
	The unit is not killed and the opponent does not draw.
</Callout>
