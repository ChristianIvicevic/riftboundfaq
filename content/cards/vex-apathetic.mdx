## Does Vex's triggered ability target the unit that was just played? [#stun-targeting]

No, as the unit to be stunned is determined automatically, so no targeting ever occurs.

When Vex's ability triggers, the unit to be stunned is determined automatically — it is always and only the unit the opponent just played.
Because no choice is ever made about which unit is stunned, the unit is not a target.<Rule number="355.10.d" />

This means targeting protection — such as "I can't be chosen by enemy spells and abilities" — offers no defense against the stun.
<Deflect /> similarly does not become relevant, since <Deflect /> is only added to a spell or ability's cost when it targets an opponent's permanent,<Rule number="809.1.d" /> which is not the case here.
