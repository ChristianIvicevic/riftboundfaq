## Does Ruined Rex's Deathknell deal damage before or after units are healed at the end of combat? [#heal-timing]

After.
The combat heal is part of the same cleanup that queues the trigger, and the trigger does not resolve until that cleanup has run to completion.

When Ruined Rex dies with lethal damage during a combat cleanup, the steps run in this order:

1. **Step 3a** — Ruined Rex's <Deathknell /> trigger is added to the chain as a pending item.<Rule number="323.4" /><Rule number="808.1.d.2" />
2. **Step 3b** — Ruined Rex is moved to the trash.<Rule number="323.5" />
3. **Step 3c** — All units are healed. This step is inserted by the combat special cleanup.<Rule number="461.1.a.1" />

The pending trigger does not begin finalization until the cleanup task is complete.<Rule number="335" />
Only then does it enter the FEPR process, get finalized (targets chosen), and resolve — dealing 4 damage to the chosen enemy unit.

## Can Ruined Rex's Deathknell target the Mech tokens created by Ferrous Forerunner's Deathknell, if both die at the same time? [#ferrous-forerunner-mechs]

No.
Targets are chosen during finalization, and [Ferrous Forerunner](https://riftbound.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/card-gallery/#card-gallery--sfd-021-221)'s Mech tokens are not created until its <Deathknell /> resolves — which only happens after all pending items have been finalized.

When both units die in the same cleanup, their <Deathknell /> triggers are queued as pending items simultaneously in turn order,<Rule number="323.4" /><Rule number="383.3.d.1" /> and items finalize in the order they were appended.<Rule number="337.1.b" />
No item resolves until all pending items have been finalized.<Rule number="337.1.c.3" />

**If Ferrous Forerunner's controller is the turn player**, their trigger is queued first and finalizes first.
The ability does not resolve yet and no Mech tokens are created.
When Ruined Rex's trigger then finalizes and must choose a target,<Rule number="355.5" /> Ferrous Forerunner's ability still has not resolved, so the Mechs do not yet exist on the board.

**If Ruined Rex's controller is the turn player**, Rex's trigger is queued first and finalizes first.
It must choose a target immediately<Rule number="355.5" /> — before Ferrous Forerunner's trigger has even finalized, let alone resolved.
Again, no Mechs exist.

Either way, once Ruined Rex's trigger has finalized, its target choice is locked in and cannot be changed.<Rule number="355.15" />
