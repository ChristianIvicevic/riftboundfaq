## Does Ruined Rex's Deathknell deal damage before or after units are healed at the end of combat? [#heal-timing]

After.
The combat heal is part of the same cleanup that queues the trigger, and the trigger does not resolve until that cleanup has run to completion.

When Ruined Rex dies with lethal damage during a combat cleanup, the steps run in this order:

1. **Step 3a** — Ruined Rex's <Deathknell /> trigger is added to the chain as a pending item.<Rule number="323.4" /><Rule number="808.1.d.2" />
2. **Step 3b** — Ruined Rex is moved to the trash.<Rule number="323.5" />
3. **Step 3c** — All units are healed. This step is inserted by the combat special cleanup.<Rule number="461.1.a.1" />

The pending trigger does not begin finalization until the cleanup task is complete.<Rule number="335" />
Only then does it enter the FEPR process, get finalized (targets chosen), and resolve — dealing 4 damage to the chosen enemy unit.
