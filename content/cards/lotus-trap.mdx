## Does Lotus Trap affect how much damage needs to be assigned in combat? [#combat-assignment]

No.

Lotus Trap is a replacement effect that applies when damage is *dealt*, not when it is *assigned*.
The combat damage sequence treats these as two separate and distinct steps.<Rule number="460.2.c.1" /><Rule number="460.2.c.1.a" />

The assignment step requires lethal damage to be assigned to a unit in full before any remaining damage may be assigned to a different unit.<Rule number="460.2.c.3" />
Lethal damage is defined as non-zero damage equalling or exceeding a unit's might — this definition makes no reference to replacement effects that apply at the time of dealing.
Lotus Trap's doubling only becomes relevant later, when damage is actually dealt.<Rule number="460.2.d" />
