## When I play Consuming Curse from my trash, does it count itself for the bonus damage? [#self-count]

No.
Playing Consuming Curse from your trash moves it onto the chain first,<Rule number="354" /><Rule number="419.1" /> so it's no longer in your trash to be counted.
Only the *other* copies still in your trash add bonus damage.

<Card name="Shadow Assassin" /> and <Card name="Shadowblade Lurker" /> work the same way.
