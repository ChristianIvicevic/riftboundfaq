## When I play Shadowblade Lurker from my trash, does it discount its own cost? [#self-count]

No.
Playing Shadowblade Lurker from your trash moves it onto the chain first,<Rule number="354" /><Rule number="419.1" /> so it's no longer in your trash when its cost is worked out.
Only the *other* copies still in your trash reduce the cost.

<Card name="Consuming Curse" /> and <Card name="Shadow Assassin" /> work the same way.
