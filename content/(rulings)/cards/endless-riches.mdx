## How long do you skip your Draw Phase? [#draw-phase-duration]

You skip every Draw Phase you would have for as long as Endless Riches remains on the board.

"Skip your draw phase" is a passive ability and a replacement effect that replaces your Draw Phase with nothing.<Rule number="364.2" /><Rule number="443.1.a" />
Passive abilities of permanents become active when the permanent enters the board and are typically active only while it remains there.<Rule number="359.2.a" /><Rule number="365.1" />
Once Endless Riches leaves the board, this ability is no longer active, so your later Draw Phases occur normally.

This is not a delayed ability that applies only to your next Draw Phase or only during a particular turn.
Delayed abilities specify the window during which they apply,<Rule number="390" /> but Endless Riches specifies no such window.
