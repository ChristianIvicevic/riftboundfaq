## Can I use Thrill of the Hunt to score on my opponent's turn by banishing my sole unit at a controlled battlefield and replaying it there? [#score-on-opponents-turn]

No, as control of the battlefield is never lost during this sequence.

When Thrill of the Hunt resolves, the replayed unit is added to the chain as a pending item,<Rule number="354.2" /> with its play process held until the spell has fully resolved.<Rule number="354.3" />
Once the spell finishes resolving, a cleanup runs — but the pending unit keeps the chain alive throughout it.<Rule number="330" /><Rule number="331" />
Because the chain is not empty, the turn remains in a closed state, and cleanup step 4 — which would make the battlefield uncontrolled — only applies during an open state.<Rule number="190.4.c" /><Rule number="323.6" />
Control is never lost, so there is no opportunity to score a point.<Rule number="469.1" />
When all outstanding tasks are complete, the pending unit is finalized<Rule number="334.2" /><Rule number="337.1" /> and, if its owner chooses that battlefield, replayed to the battlefield you still control.<Rule number="359.2" />
