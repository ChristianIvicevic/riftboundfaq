## When I play Consuming Curse from my trash, does it count itself for the bonus damage? [#self-count]

No.
[A card leaves your trash for the chain as soon as you begin playing it](/general-rules/playing-cards#playing-from-trash), so Consuming Curse cannot count itself.
Only the *other* copies still in your trash add bonus damage.
