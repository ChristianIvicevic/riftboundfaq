## Does Abandoned Hall's ability trigger if the spell is countered? [#countered-spell]

No.
Abandoned Hall's ability triggers "when a player plays a spell," and a countered spell is not considered played.

A card is only played once the entire play process has finished in its entirety.<Rule number="350.1" />
Abilities that trigger on playing a card fire when that act has been completed by the card's resolution;<Rule number="419.4.a" /> if a game effect prevents resolution — for example because the spell was countered — the card is not considered to have been played and no abilities that trigger on playing cards fire.<Rule number="419.4.a.1" />
This follows directly from what countering does: a countered card is cleared from the chain and is not considered to have been played.<Rule number="425.1.b" />

## Can a unit moved into or away from Abandoned Hall by a spell be chosen? [#movement-eligibility]

Only if it is at Abandoned Hall after the spell fully resolves.

A card is only considered "played" once the entire play process has finished,<Rule number="350.1" /> which for spells means after all instructions have been executed and the card is placed in the trash.
Abandoned Hall's triggered ability therefore fires after the spell resolves, not when it is placed on the chain.<Rule number="419.4.a" /><Rule number="383.2.c" />
By then, a unit moved into Abandoned Hall is present and can be chosen, while a unit moved away no longer qualifies as "a unit they control here."

## Can a unit replayed here by a spell be chosen by Abandoned Hall's triggered ability? [#replay]

Yes.
The replayed unit enters the board before Abandoned Hall's triggered ability chooses its target, so it is a valid choice for the buff.

### Resolution Sequence [#replay-sequence]

1. A spell such as <Card name="Arcane Shift" /> adds the replayed unit to the chain as a pending item while it resolves.<Rule number="354.2" /><Rule number="354.3" />
2. When that spell finishes resolving, Abandoned Hall's triggered ability becomes pending after the unit.<Rule number="350.1" /><Rule number="419.4.a" /><Rule number="383.2.c" />
3. Required cleanups and other outstanding tasks finish before either pending item can be finalized.<Rule number="334.1" />
4. The [older pending unit finalizes first](/general-rules/chain-and-priority#pending-item-order) and immediately enters the board.<Rule number="337.2" />
5. Abandoned Hall's triggered ability finalizes next and can choose the replayed unit.
