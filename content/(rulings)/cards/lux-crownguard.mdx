## Can I use Lux, Crownguard's ability to pay for Hard Bargain? [#hard-bargain-payment]

No.
Lux's ability can add energy to pay a spell's costs while that spell is being played, but it cannot supply the energy demanded by <Card name="Hard Bargain" />.

Paying a spell's costs is part of the process of playing it.
During that step, its controller can activate <Reaction /> abilities that add resources to pay those costs.<Rule number="357.1" /><Rule number="357.1.a" />
Once the spell is finalized on the chain, its costs have already been paid and opponents receive an opportunity to play reactions.<Rule number="359.3.a" /><Rule number="359.3.c" />

Hard Bargain's instruction, "Counter a spell unless its controller pays <Energy value={2} />," is executed later, when Hard Bargain resolves.<Rule number="158.1" />
That payment is not a cost of playing the targeted spell.
Although abilities that add resources can normally be activated whenever a spell or ability requires resources to be paid, Lux restricts her added energy to playing spells.<Rule number="429.3" />
It therefore cannot be used for Hard Bargain's payment or for another payment required while a spell or ability resolves.
