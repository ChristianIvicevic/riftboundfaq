## Does Sunken Temple trigger if I conquer with a unit that is only Mighty due to Assault? [#mighty-from-assault]

Yes, Sunken Temple will trigger even if the unit is only <Mighty /> because of the bonus might it gained from <Assault />.<Rule number="807.1.c" /><Rule number="807.1.d.1" />

Control is established and the conquer occurs<Rule number="466.5.d" /> before attacker and defender designations are removed.<Rule number="466.7.a" />
This means units still have their attacker designation — and therefore <Assault /> active — at the time the conquer trigger is evaluated.<Rule number="708" /><Rule number="710" />
The requirement to conquer with a <Mighty /> unit is part of Sunken Temple's [trigger condition](/general-rules/abilities#trigger-time-conditions) and is checked at that moment.
