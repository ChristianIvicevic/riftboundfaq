## Can I use Arcane Shift to banish my only unit at a battlefield and replay it there? [#replay-at-same-battlefield]

Yes, as long as you control the battlefield.

When Arcane Shift resolves, the replayed unit sits on the chain as a pending item throughout the cleanup that follows.
[A pending chain item prevents the battlefield from becoming uncontrolled during that cleanup](/general-rules/chain-and-priority#empty-battlefield-control), so when the unit is finalized you can choose to replay it there.
Attackers cannot replay their unit to the attacking battlefield because they never control it in the first place.

### Resolution Sequence [#replay-sequence]

When Arcane Shift resolves, the following happens:

1. "Banish a friendly unit" — the unit leaves the board.
2. "then its owner plays it, ignoring its cost" — the unit is added to the chain as a pending item,<Rule number="354.2" /> but its play process pauses while Arcane Shift continues resolving.<Rule number="354.3" />
3. "Deal 3 to an enemy unit" and "Banish this" both resolve.
4. Arcane Shift finishes resolving, queuing one or more cleanups as outstanding tasks.<Rule number="321.1" />
5. The cleanups run.
The pending unit keeps the battlefield controlled throughout them.
6. Once all outstanding tasks are complete, the pending unit is finalized.<Rule number="334.2" />
You choose where to play it,<Rule number="355.2" /> and the battlefield is still under your control.
