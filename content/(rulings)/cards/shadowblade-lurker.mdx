## When I play Shadowblade Lurker from my trash, does it discount its own cost? [#self-count]

No.
[A card leaves your trash for the chain as soon as you begin playing it](/general-rules/playing-cards#playing-from-trash), so Shadowblade Lurker cannot count itself while its cost is determined.
Only the *other* copies still in your trash reduce the cost.
