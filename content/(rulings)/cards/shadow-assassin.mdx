## Does Shadow Assassin count itself when I play it from my trash? [#self-count]

No.
Playing Shadow Assassin from your trash moves it onto the chain first,<Rule number="354" /><Rule number="419.1" /> so by the time it enters, it's no longer in your trash.
It enters ready only if a *different* copy of it is still there.

<Card name="Consuming Curse" /> and <Card name="Shadowblade Lurker" /> work the same way.
