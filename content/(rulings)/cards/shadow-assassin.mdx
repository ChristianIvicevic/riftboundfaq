## When I play Shadow Assassin from my trash, does it enter ready off its own copy? [#self-count]

No.
[A card leaves your trash for the chain as soon as you begin playing it](/general-rules/playing-cards#playing-from-trash), so Shadow Assassin cannot count itself.
It enters ready only if a *different* copy of it is still there.
