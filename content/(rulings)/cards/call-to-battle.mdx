## Can I choose a unit's current battlefield as Call to Battle's destination? [#same-battlefield-destination]

No, because Call to Battle requires the player to choose the move destination, so the restriction on valid destinations applies directly.

The [chosen destination must differ from the unit's current location](/general-rules/movement#chosen-destination).
Call to Battle has the player choose both the unit and the battlefield, so the chosen battlefield cannot be the unit's current location.
If no valid destination can be chosen for the selected unit, the spell cannot be finalized.

This contrasts with <Card name="Flash" />, which specifies base as its destination and can target a unit already there.
