## Which costs does Ezreal reduce? [#optional-additional-costs]

Ezreal reduces only optional additional costs you choose to pay.
These costs use both the word "may" and the exact phrase "as an additional cost."<Rule number="356.2.b.1" />
<Accelerate /> and <Repeat /> are keyworded optional additional costs, so Ezreal reduces them too.<Rule number="805.1.a" /><Rule number="820.1.d" />

Ezreal's discount applies to the optional additional cost itself as soon as that cost is added, before discounts that apply to the card's total cost.<Rule number="356.4.c" /><Rule number="356.4.d" />

Mandatory additional costs are not optional because they omit "may,"<Rule number="356.2.a.1" /> and payments made while carrying out an effect are not additional costs to play that card.
Ezreal does not reduce either kind of payment.

<Callout type="idea" title="Example">
	<Card name="Blast Corps Cadet" /> says "You may pay <Energy value={1} /><Fury /> as an additional cost to play me."
	Ezreal can reduce that optional additional cost to either <Energy value={1} /> or <Fury />.
	By contrast, Ezreal does not reduce <Card name="Cruel Patron" />'s mandatory cost to kill a friendly unit or the <Energy value={2} /> an opponent may pay during <Card name="Hard Bargain" />'s effect.
	Ezreal does reduce Hard Bargain's separate <Repeat /> cost.
</Callout>
