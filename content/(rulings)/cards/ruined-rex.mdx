## Does Ruined Rex's Deathknell deal damage before or after units are healed at the end of combat? [#heal-timing]

After.

Ruined Rex follows the [normal timing for a Deathknell ability created during a cleanup](/mechanics/deathknell#cleanup-timing): its trigger becomes pending when Rex dies, but the combat cleanup finishes before that ability is finalized.
Surviving units are therefore healed before its target is chosen and it deals 4 damage.<Rule number="466.1.a.1" />

## Can Ruined Rex's Deathknell target the Mech tokens created by Ferrous Forerunner's Deathknell, if both die at the same time? [#ferrous-forerunner-mechs]

No.
Ruined Rex chooses its target before <Card name="Ferrous Forerunner" /> creates its Mech tokens.

When both units die in the same cleanup, their <Deathknell /> abilities become pending items.<Rule number="323.4" />
[All pending items are finalized before players receive priority and abilities begin resolving](/general-rules/chain-and-priority#pending-item-order).
Ruined Rex therefore chooses its target before Ferrous Forerunner creates its Mechs, regardless of finalization order.<Rule number="355.5" />
