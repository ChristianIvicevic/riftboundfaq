## Do opponents have to pay resources to play cards from Hidden while Vex, Cheerless is in combat? [#hidden-cost-increase]

Yes, cards played from Hidden by your opponents still cost <Energy value={1} /><Universal /> when Vex is in combat.

When a card with Hidden is played from its facedown state, it is played "ignoring its base cost".<Rule number="811.1.b" />
During step 3 of playing a card ("Determine Total Cost"), the following happens in order:

1. Set base costs to zero.<Rule number="356.1" />
If an ability or instruction allows you to "ignore" costs, set the base costs to zero.
Hidden cards played from facedown have their base cost set to <Energy value={0} />.
2. Apply cost increases.<Rule number="356.3" />
Vex's ability increases enemy spell costs by <Energy value={1} /><Universal />, which is applied to the total cost even though the base cost is zero.
3. Apply discounts.<Rule number="356.4" />
No discounts apply in this scenario.

## Does Vex, Cheerless reduce Deflect costs when you play spells? [#deflect-cost-reduction]

Yes, the cost reduction applies to <Deflect /> costs when you target an opponent's permanent while she's in combat.

<Deflect /> is a mandatory additional cost.<Rule number="356.2.a.2" /><Rule number="809.1.d" />
When you play a friendly spell that targets an opponent's permanent with <Deflect />, that additional cost is added before Vex's discount is applied.<Rule number="356.2" /><Rule number="356.4" />

Vex's discount can offset resources added by <Deflect /> because discounts can reduce additional costs.<Rule number="356.4.f" />
However, her discount cannot reduce the spell's resulting cost below her printed minimum of <Energy value={1} />.<Rule number="356.4.e" />
