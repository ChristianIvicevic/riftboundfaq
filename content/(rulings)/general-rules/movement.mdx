## Does a unit move when it enters the board? [#entering-board]

No.

Moving means changing a permanent's position from one space on the board to another.<Rule number="446.1" />
A card entering the board from another game zone is a zone change, not a move.<Rule number="446.2" />

Playing or creating a unit at a battlefield therefore does not fulfill an ability that triggers when the unit moves there.
Replacing where a unit enters the board also does not turn that entry into a move.

## Can I choose a unit's current location as its move destination? [#chosen-destination]

No.

When an effect requires a player to choose a move destination for a unit, that destination must differ from the unit's current location.<Rule number="355.4" /><Rule number="355.4.a" />
If the card specifies the destination instead, the player makes no destination choice and this restriction does not affect whether the unit is a legal target.
If the unit is already at that specified destination when the instruction resolves, the move is impossible and is ignored.<Rule number="055" />
